Your Guide to the World of Role-Playing Games

START YOUR RPG JOURNEY!

Roleplaying games (RPGs) let you and your friends step into the roles of heroes in a shared story. Together, you create characters, battle monsters, uncover mysteries, and forge memories that last a lifetime.

Dungeons & Dragons is the most famous RPG, but it's just one of many ways to play. Whether you're new to fantasy adventures or curious about tabletop gaming, The Adventurer's Codex will guide you step by step.

WHAT ARE ROLEPLAYING GAMES?

In roleplaying games, players form an adventuring party that explores fantastic worlds, embarks on quests, and grows stronger over time.

The magic of RPGs is that no two stories are the same. Every roll of the dice and every decision from the players weaves a tale of triumph, tragedy, and laughter you'll remember long after the game ends.
