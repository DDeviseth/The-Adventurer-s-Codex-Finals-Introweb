Your Guide to the World of Role-Playing Games

START YOUR RPG JOURNEY!

Roleplaying games (RPGs) let you and your friends step into the roles of heroes in a shared story. Together, you create characters, battle monsters, uncover mysteries, and forge memories that last a lifetime.

Dungeons & Dragons is the most famous RPG, but it’s just one of many ways to play. Whether you’re new to fantasy adventures or curious about tabletop gaming, The Adventurer’s Codex will guide you step by step.

Learn More in Getting Started

WHAT ARE ROLEPLAYING GAMES?

In roleplaying games, players form an adventuring party that explores fantastic worlds, embarks on quests, and grows stronger over time. One person serves as the Game Master (or Dungeon Master in D&D), who runs the story, controls non-player characters, and sets the challenges.

The magic of RPGs is that no two stories are the same. Every roll of the dice and every decision from the players weaves a tale of triumph, tragedy, and laughter you’ll remember long after the game ends.

LEARN THE RULES OF PLAY

RPGs use a mix of imagination and dice rolls to decide what happens. The rules explain how skills work, how to fight monsters, how to cast magic, and what conditions affect your hero during an adventure.

CREATE YOUR CHARACTER

Every adventure begins with a hero of your own making. You’ll decide their race, class, background, and ability scores, shaping not only their skills but also their personality and story.

Your character is more than numbers—it’s the role you bring to life at the table.

EQUIP YOUR HERO, EXPLORE THE WORLD

Adventurers need more than courage. Weapons, armor, tools, and magic items help you survive dungeons, fight monsters, and claim treasure. Along the way, you’ll discover strange creatures, forgotten realms, and even other planes of existence.

The world of RPGs is vast, and part of the joy is exploring it piece by piece.
