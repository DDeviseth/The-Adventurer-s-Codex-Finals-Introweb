Character Selection

Characters

Your race defines what kind of being your character isâ€”human, elf, dwarf, halfling, and so on. Each race grants special traits like darkvision, natural resilience, or a bonus to ability scores.

Humans

Versatile and adaptable.

Elves

Graceful, long-lived, and skilled in magic.

Dwarves

Tough, wise, and resistant to poison.

Halflings

Nimble and lucky.

Dragonborn

Carry the power of dragons, with breath weapons.

Tieflings

Have infernal heritage, marked by horns or tails.

Half-Orcs

Fierce and resilient, able to endure deadly blows.

Gnomes

Clever and inventive, often tied to magic.

Half-Elves

Blend human adaptability with elven charm.

Classes

Your class determines your character's role, skills, and powers. Each class has unique features that grow stronger as you level up.

Barbarian

Fearless warriors fueled by rage.

Bard

Masters of music, magic, and inspiration.

Cleric

Holy champions who wield divine power.

Druid

Guardians of nature who can shape-shift into animals.

Fighter

Skilled combatants with weapons and tactics.

Monk

Martial artists who channel inner ki.

Paladin

Oath-bound knights with divine smite.

Ranger

Trackers and hunters attuned to the wild.

Rogue

Masters of stealth, trickery, and precision strikes.

Sorcerer

Spellcasters powered by innate magic.

Warlock

Wielders of dark gifts from otherworldly patrons.

Wizard

Scholars who master spells through study.

Ability Scores

Every character has six ability scores that define their strengths and weaknesses. When creating a character, you assign numbers to these scores. Higher numbers mean better bonuses in related actions.

Strength

Physical power, lifting, athletics.

Dexterity

Agility, stealth, accuracy.

Constitution

Endurance, toughness, stamina.

Intelligence

Knowledge, logic, spell study.

Wisdom

Awareness, insight, perception.

Charisma

Influence, charm, leadership.

Backgrounds

Your background tells the story of who your character was before they became an adventurer. Backgrounds grant extra skills, tool proficiencies, and roleplaying flavor.

Acolyte

Raised in a temple, knowledgeable in religion.

Criminal

Skilled in stealth, deception, and thieves' tools.

Folk Hero

A humble hero of the people, known for bravery.

Noble

Born to high status, influential and persuasive.

Sage

A learned scholar, versed in history and arcana.

Soldier

Trained in battle, disciplined and commanding.
